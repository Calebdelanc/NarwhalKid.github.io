Types of chemical reactions

By: Billy Jellig



What Are the Parts of a Chemical Equation?


Reactants - The chemicals being combined in the the chemical reaction
Products - The new chemical being made in the chemical reaction
Subscript - The little number to the right and below an element. This represents the number of atoms in each molecule
Coefficient - The number to the left of a molecule. This represents the number of total molecules

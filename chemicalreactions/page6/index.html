Types of chemical reactions

By: Billy Jellig



How Do You Identify Each Chemical Reaction? (click on each answer to be brought to a picture or video!)


Synthesis - Occurs when two different atoms or molecules combine to form a different molecule
Decomposition - Occurs when one molecule breaks up into two or more molecules
Single Displacement - Occurs when one element is replaced by another in a compound
Double Displacement - Occurs when two elements are replace by another 2 in a compound
